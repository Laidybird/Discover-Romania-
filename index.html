Romania's Capital is Bucharest

Bucharest, in southern Romania, is the country's capital and commercial center. Its iconic landmark is the massive, communist-era Palatul Parlamentului government building, which has 1,100 rooms.

Dacia Cars are Made in Romania

Dacia was created in Romania in 1966, with a clear objective: to provide modern, reliable and affordable vehicles to the people of Romania. It was dubbed Dacia after the name given by the Romans.

Danube Delta

Amongst the highlights are such classic birds of the Danube Delta as White Pelican and the endangered Dalmatian Pelican, Pygmy Cormorants, White-tailed Eagles and Marsh Harriers, Purple and Squacco Herons, Bitterns and Little Bitterns.
